This web map visualizes population and employment growth between 2020 and 2045 at the traffic analysis zone (TAZ) level in the DVRPC region.

About this data

DVRPC's Long Range Plan identifies population and employment forecasts for every 5th year between 2015 and 2050. This map highlights the total and percent change in population and employment between 2020 and 2045 at the TAZ level, with an overlay of SEPTA's Regional Rail network.
